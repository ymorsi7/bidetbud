About HalalBud

Halal food when you're far from home

Traveling in the West means constantly asking: is the meat halal? In Muslim-majority countries, meat is halal by default — those countries are shaded green on the map.

Everywhere else, HalalBud shows restaurants we've found with explicit halal evidence — either fully halal (everything is halal) or halal options (mixed menu with halal dishes).

We only add places with a cited source. Know a spot? Tap Add restaurant.
