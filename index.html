About BidetBud

A community map for bidet-friendly spots

I have a habit of getting annoyed at minor inconveniences and then trying to build a solution for them.

As someone who travels regularly between the US and the Middle East, I noticed one thing the West evidently still hasn't figured out: bidets.

In the Middle East, bidets are something you just don't have to think about — a convenience we don't realize we're spoiled with until we return to the West.

This has been ruffling my feathers for years, so I built BidetBud: a free map where you can find masajid, restaurants, hotels, and public spots that actually have bidets / washlets across the US, Canada, and the UK.

There are hundreds of pins on the map already, but its usefulness grows with every spot the community adds. If you know one, add it — it takes a minute. Name, address, done.
